    Yes, probably more than you realize.  The term originated in psycho-analysis circles, to describe any person that had a significant influence on another person.  This included spouse/ boyfriend/ girlfriend, but could also be parents, siblings, roomates, co-workers etc.  Eventually, popular culture ceased on the term, because it was a good euphormism for "person you are living with but aren’t married to", and it’s meaning got limited to just romantic partners.
