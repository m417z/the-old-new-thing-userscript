      And the reason that they use a diaresis on coordinate is that it makes sense. In English, a diaresis on the second vowel of a pair of vowels (for example “noël” indicates that the two vowels should be pronounced separately (like “no-ell” rather than “noal”). Without the diaresis, “coordinate” really should have the “oo” pronounced like the “oo” in either “foot” or “food”. However, nearly no one cares.
