Xformer 10: The Atari 800 emulator has gotten a huge update

Summary:It's over 30 years old, but can still be improved, and it sure did.

Emulator master Darek Mihocka hasn't been resting on his laurels. He and fellow Atari 8-bit developer Danny Miller have released Xformer 10, a massive revamp of their Xformer Atari 800 emulator.
