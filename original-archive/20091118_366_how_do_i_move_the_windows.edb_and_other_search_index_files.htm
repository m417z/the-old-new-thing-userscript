Summary:Nothing profound today, just a little tip. My customer is looking out for a way to change the location of the windows.edb file to another (larger) drive. From the Indexing Options Control Panel, click Advanced, and then under Index location, click Select new.

Nothing profound today, just a little tip.

My customer is looking out for a way to change the location of the windows.edb file to another (larger) drive.

From the Indexing Options Control Panel, click Advanced, and then under Index location, click Select new.
