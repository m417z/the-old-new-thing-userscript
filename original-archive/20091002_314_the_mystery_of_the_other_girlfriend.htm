  • Friend 1's "other girlfriend" is a male colleague with whom he often goes to dinner when working late.

  • Friend 3's "other girlfriend" is a "regularly changing set of small electronic devices."

I don't know for certain, but if you ask my wife, she'll probably say that my "other girlfriend" is this Web site.
